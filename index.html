About Madrid RR App

Madrid RR is a mission-critical tool designed for the IPC GSOC Madrid site to streamline ticket assignment and team coordination.

Core Features

  • Smart Round Robin: Automatic and fair distribution of tickets among active team members.
  • Load Balancing: Tickets are always assigned to the available person with the fewest tickets across both shifts, ensuring even workload distribution.
  • Teams Integration: Automated notifications to assigned members, ensuring no ticket is missed.
  • Real-time Sync: Full cloud persistence via Firebase for a single source of truth across all team members.
  • Multi-Timezone Support: Facilitates seamless coordination and visualization between team members across both Madrid and New York regions.
